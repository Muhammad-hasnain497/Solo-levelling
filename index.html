Arise

Jin-Woo

The main protagonist of Solo Leveling. Sung Jin-woo transforms from humanity's weakest hunter to a powerful combatant through the System. Sung's selflessness and dedication to family contrast with his dark turn to violence post-awakening. Despite public acclaim and growth, Sung remains curious about his powers' origins and the System's intentions.

About Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling is a South Korean web novel written by Chugong. It follows the journey of Jin-Woo, the weakest hunter, as he rises to become the strongestSolo Leveling, also alternatively translated as Only I Level Up (Korean: 나 혼자만 레벨업; RR: Na Honjaman Rebeleop), is a South Korean portal fantasy[1] web novel written by Chugong. It was serialized in Kakao's digital comic and fiction platform KakaoPage beginning on July 25, 2016, and was later published by D&C Media under their Papyrus label since November 4, 2016. The novel has been licensed in English by Yen Press.[2].
